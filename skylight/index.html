Product Management

Skylight started out as an augmented reality software. I expanded Skylight's offerings through watch app release and private beta launch of wearables starter kit, generating new sales leads from supply chain management consultancies including Jabil.

I gathered API requirements and product usage data points to integrate with GE, Boeing, ServiceMax, and Salesforce. I defined KPIs for manufacturing, logistics, oil and gas, and field service by collaborating with engineering, sales, and operations teams.

When shipping and plannng products, I use the Kano model to make everyone happy.

UI/UX Design

I jump right into design when situations demand. I am proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, and other prototyping tools.

Wearables is an interesting interface where traditional approaches such as wireframing does not guarante the best results. Understanding the principles of decision-making, cognitive recognition, and cognitive recall is important. To learn more about human factors, see healthcare page.

I designed watch app user flow and voice UI which increased productivity and resource utilization by 20-25% and decreased error rate by 30%.
