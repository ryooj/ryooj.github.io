Skylight

Since 2010, APX has been the leader in developing wearables based solutions for the industrial workforce. APX provides Skylight to Global 1000 enterprises in manufacturing, logistics, oil & gas and field services with active users in the US, Europe and Middle East. I worked for APX as a Product Manager.

APX Website

Due to the nature of the job, work details are not public. This post is dedicated to general descriptions of what I did as a Product Manager at APX.

Product management and Strategy

All about Scrum. I define features; write specs and user stories; create wire frames, prototypes, test plans and release checklists; and manage product backlog. I work with cross-functional teams including Engineering, Design, Sales, Operations and QA.

Tools - Github, Github Pages, Hansoft, Zendesk, Jira.

UI/UX Design

Overall responsibility for building new Skylight app for web and mobile platform, and creating out-of-the-box reports that inform the success of the product. Watch prototypes, survey and release plan. Voice UI.

Tools - Adobe Creative Cloud, Photoshop, Illustrator, AfterEffect, Sketch, Marvel.

Marketing

I wrote release documentation, and created product materials and brand assets to enhance the sales and operations representatives' understanding of complex product features and help them sell and train customers more effectively.

Tools - Technical Writing Skill, Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch.

Research and Development

I worked in interdisciplinary teams to build in-house demo station to showcase Skylight.
