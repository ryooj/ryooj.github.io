Medical Devices

To make medical devices "work", you must understand the entire system and stakeholders. Below summarizes the process:

    1. You must obtain FDA pre-market approval (for devices), meaning it should be "safe and effective" (Class III device) or "substantially equivalent" (510(k)).
    2. You must have third party payers agree to pay.
    3. You must convince physicians (or other relevant stakeholders) to use.

My two main projects were:

  • Pressure sensing system for pediatric ventilation masks. (IRB approval preparation stage)
  • Optogenetic approches to retinal prosthesis. See here to view details.
