Human Factors

If interested in issues around automotive HUDs, read this paper.

A general approach is how to design a perfect secondary information source screen. Parallel processing of objects in two perceptual groups - static screen and dynamic background - is difficult to achieve because visual and spatial attention is a limited resource.

HUD can avoid attentional capture by segregating perceptual modes and allow drivers to see information in peripheral vision.
